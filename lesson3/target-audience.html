Target audience: people interested in the weather and the community who are planning for fun.

Example personas:
1. Retiree couple
2. Mother with young children

-looking for some local fun but do not want to get caught in the rain
-trying to decide where to schedule the playgroup
-plans cancelled due to the weather and looking for a new option
-wants to figure out where the crowds will be on the weekend
-hoping for a good kite-flying day
